About M77 AG

Family Business • Four Generations • Haxtun Heritage

Four Generations of American Agriculture

M77 AG represents four generations of American agricultural excellence. Our family has been farming in Haxtun since the homesteading era, continuing a tradition that spans over a century of dedicated stewardship of the land.

Located in the heart of one of Colorado's most productive farming regions, we've adapted and evolved with modern agriculture while maintaining the values and work ethic passed down through generations. Haxtun sits at the crossroads of US Highway 6 and Highway 59, serving as the agricultural hub for Northeast Colorado where many families continue to farm land homesteaded by their relatives more than 100 years ago.

Today, we continue this tradition to the best of our abilities, combining time-tested farming wisdom with cutting-edge agricultural technology. Our operation features multiple test plots where we conduct ongoing research with chemicals, fertilizers, and crop varieties, ensuring we stay at the forefront of agricultural innovation while honoring our heritage.
