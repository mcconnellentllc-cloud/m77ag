Your All-Access Hunting Experience

The M77 AG Heritage Farm offers some of our best all-around hunting across 1,160 acres of diverse habitat in Sedgwick County, Colorado. Hunt the same land our Swedish ancestors homesteaded in the late 1800s, now optimized for trophy wildlife and unforgettable hunting experiences.

Colorado DOW Big Game Unit: 93

IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR DOG HUNTERS

Crop Ground Conditions: Due to the abundance of rain this season, crop ground areas are heavily loaded with sandburs and goat heads (stickers). If your dogs are not accustomed to running in sticker patches, it is strongly advised to stay in the native grass areas. Native grass sections are sticker-free and provide excellent hunting opportunities.

Diverse Terrain

From rolling dunes and native tallgrass prairie to CRP fields, multiple crop types, and established tree shelterbelts - every parcel offers unique hunting opportunities.

Year-Round Water

Reliable water sources throughout the property attract and hold wildlife year-round, creating consistent hunting opportunities across all seasons.

Premium Habitat

Extensive fencelines, grass borders, and surrounding habitat provide exceptional cover and travel corridors, making this some of our best all-around hunting ground.

Historic Camping

Camp at the original homestead site with water and 110V electric hookups. Experience the same land four generations of our family have called home.

Five Parcels

Rolling Dunes, Prairie Parcel, Homestead, North Side, and South Side - each parcel offers distinct terrain and hunting opportunities within one all-access package.

Trophy Potential

Mature mule deer, abundant pheasants in tall grass, mourning doves at one of the area's best hunting spots, coyotes, and occasional elk migrations make this property a multi-species paradise.

Five Historic Parcels

Rolling Dunes

240 acres

True to its name, this parcel features undulating terrain with native grasses and strategic tree plantings. The varied topography creates natural funnels and bedding areas for deer, while providing excellent cover for pheasants. Perfect for spot-and-stalk hunting with multiple vantage points.

Prairie Parcel

320 acres

Expansive tallgrass prairie interspersed with CRP fields and crop edges. This is classic Great Plains hunting at its finest, offering long-range opportunities for deer and exceptional pheasant habitat. The open terrain allows for visibility while maintaining pockets of cover that hold birds and game throughout the season.

Sedgwick County Homestead

200 acres

The heart of our Swedish heritage - the original homestead site from the late 1800s. This parcel combines native grass, tree shelterbelts, and year-round water sources. Mature trees provide roosting areas while open fields offer feeding grounds. Known as one of the area's best dove hunting spots. The camping area is located here with water and 110V electric hookups. Hunt where four generations hunted before you.

North Side

180 acres

A diverse mix of native grass, tall grass, CRP, crops, and established tree lines. This parcel excels at holding deer and pheasants due to its varied habitat structure. The combination of food sources, cover, and water creates an ideal environment for wildlife. Occasional elk have been spotted passing through this area during migration.

South Side

220 acres

Similar in character to the North Side, this parcel features native grass, tall grass, CRP fields, multiple crops, and protective tree shelterbelts. Extensive fencelines and bordering habitat create natural travel corridors. The variety of habitat types attracts diverse wildlife and provides excellent hunting opportunities year-round.

Ideal for Medium to Large Groups

With 1,160 acres of diverse terrain across five distinct parcels, the M77 AG Heritage Farm is perfectly suited for groups of hunters looking for a comprehensive hunting experience. The property can comfortably accommodate multiple hunting parties simultaneously, with each group finding their own space across the various parcels.

Recommended: 6-12 hunters

The size and diversity of our parcels make this an excellent choice for hunting clubs, family groups, or corporate outings. Whether you're organizing a pheasant drive across the Prairie Parcel or setting up deer stands throughout the property, there's ample space and habitat for everyone to enjoy a quality hunt.

Four Generations of Swedish Heritage

Our Swedish ancestors first homesteaded this land in the late 1800s, breaking sod and building a life on the Colorado prairie. Today, you can camp at the very site where they built their home, surrounded by the same rolling hills and grasslands they knew. Over four generations, our family has carefully stewarded this land, creating the perfect balance of agricultural production and wildlife habitat.

The Heritage Farm Experience

When you hunt the M77 AG Heritage Farm, you're not just accessing 1,160 acres of prime habitat - you're stepping into a legacy that spans four generations of family stewardship.
