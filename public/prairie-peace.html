Small Group Paradise

Prairie Peace offers a unique hunting experience perfectly suited for solo hunters or small groups of 3-4 people. Located south of Haxtun in peaceful Logan County, this 1,550-acre property provides the intimacy and variety that larger operations can't match. Very peaceful with minimal traffic - hunt birds at dawn, shoot prairie dogs all day, and return to nesting zones at dusk for an unforgettable full-day adventure.

Colorado DOW Big Game Unit: 98

IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR DOG HUNTERS

Crop Ground Conditions: Due to the abundance of rain this season, crop ground areas are heavily loaded with sandburs and goat heads (stickers). If your dogs are not accustomed to running in sticker patches, it is strongly advised to stay in the native grass areas. Native grass sections are sticker-free and provide excellent hunting opportunities.

Small Group Focus

Designed for 3-4 hunters, Prairie Peace offers an intimate experience without the crowds. No large grass fields requiring big groups - just quality hunting for those who prefer a personal touch.

Dawn to Dusk Variety

Start with bird hunting at sunrise, transition to prairie dog shooting through midday, then return to nesting zones for evening hunts. Non-stop action from sunup to sundown.

Seven Diverse Parcels

From creek bottoms perfect for doves to open prairie teeming with prairie dogs, each of our seven parcels offers unique terrain and hunting opportunities across 1,550 acres.

Prairie Dog Haven

Lots of prairie dogs - our main attraction! Active colonies provide challenging long-range shooting all day long. Perfect for honing your skills between morning and evening bird hunts.

Creek Bottom Hotspot

The Creek Bed on 14 parcel is prime dove and pheasant territory. Natural waterways and vegetation create ideal habitat for exceptional bird hunting opportunities.

Peace & Quiet

True to its name - very peaceful location with minimal traffic. Located south of Haxtun, you'll enjoy solitude and serenity rarely found on hunting properties.

Prairie Dog Paradise

The centerpiece of Prairie Peace is our extensive prairie dog colonies. These active towns provide hours of challenging long-range shooting opportunities throughout the day when other game is bedded down.

Seven Diverse Parcels

The South Section

598 acres

Our largest parcel offering open prairie and prime prairie dog colonies. Excellent deer movement along edges and plenty of room for long-range shooting practice.

Prairie Peace Campsite Acreage

155 acres

Mixed terrain with good cover for deer and turkey. The varied landscape creates natural funnels and provides excellent ambush points for multiple species.

Creek Bed on 14

169 acres

Prime dove and pheasant habitat! The creek bottom provides natural water and vegetation that attracts birds throughout the day. This is your go-to spot for exceptional wing shooting.

Grass and Fenceline

316 acres

Strategic fencelines and grass provide natural travel corridors for deer and excellent pheasant cover. The mix of open areas and structure makes this parcel highly productive.

Fencelines

162 acres

Named for its prominent fence structures that create perfect game corridors. Ideal for setting up and waiting for deer during morning and evening movements.

Logan County Trees

151 acres

Tree-lined areas provide roosting habitat for turkeys and shelter for deer. The contrast between wooded areas and open ground creates dynamic hunting opportunities.
