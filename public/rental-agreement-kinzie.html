2. Premises

Landlord leases to Tenants the residential property located at 168 Hwy 59, Sedgwick, Colorado 80749 (the "Premises"), a 2-bedroom, 1-bath single-family home including the enclosed porch, yard, and off-street parking. The Premises includes stove, microwave, and washer/dryer hookups. A refrigerator is NOT included and shall be supplied by Tenants.

3. Term of Lease

This lease begins on July 1, 2026 and ends on June 30, 2027 (12 months). If Tenants remain in possession after expiration without a new signed agreement, tenancy shall continue on a month-to-month basis at Landlord's then-current market rate.
