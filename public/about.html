Our Heritage

M77 AG's roots trace back to the late 1800s when our Swedish ancestors first homesteaded in Sedgwick County, Colorado. What began as a simple family farm has evolved into a comprehensive agricultural operation.

Today, Kyle and Brandi McConnell continue the family tradition, combining time-tested farming practices with modern technology to serve Northeast Colorado's agricultural community.

1890s
Swedish ancestors establish original homestead in Sedgwick County
1920s
Family expands operations, begins custom farming services
1980s
Modernization with new equipment and farming techniques
2020s
Kyle and Brandi launch M77 AG, integrating technology and tradition
